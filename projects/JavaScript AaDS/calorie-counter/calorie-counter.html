Calorie Counter Project

This project is a simple calorie counter. First, you start with your budget. This is the target calories that you want to measure your surplus/deficit by. Then, you click the dropdown to select what category of food you want to add, input the name of the item, then the calories of said item, and it will add it to the list.

Once you are satisfied with your list, you can click the 'Calculate Remaining Calories' button to see if you are in a deficit or surplus, and by how much. You will get details on your original target paired with your consumption, as well as how many calories you burned during exercise (if any!).

This one was really cool, as it was sort of an introduction for me to the way to expand or collapse information interactively.
