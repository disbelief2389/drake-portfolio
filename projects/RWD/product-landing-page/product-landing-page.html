Product Landing Page Project

This one was pretty fun. I learned about navbars, iframes, the fixed CSS position and many other things as to how to make a website look like a product page.

Our task was to choose a product and make a product landing page for it. I chose the TicWatch Pro 5. I had to make a navbar, a video, and a few sections with features, specifications, and contact information.

Pretty basic stuff now that I look back at it, but it was an essential learning experience.
