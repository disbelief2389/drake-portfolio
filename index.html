About

v3i1ix (Drake Hay) is a critic of oppressive systems, particularly the LDS Church, which he was born into. His Patreon supports those questioning religious indoctrination. Born to a Mormon family, his childhood was shattered at age two when his father witnessed his mother’s sexual assault in church. She later vilified his father, divorced him, and remarried, perpetuating lies about his character. Drake condemns societal hypocrisy that scapegoats men until they’re driven to suicide—a fate he narrowly escaped.

Rejecting complacency, he challenges institutions that prioritize control and profit (e.g., the LDS Church’s tithing system) over humanity. His DMT experiences inspired his alias, v3i1ix, reflecting peeled-back "veils" of illusion. He condemns child abuse, societal apathy, and the comfort-driven ignorance of suffering, quoting Terence McKenna: “Control is what they’re after.” Unfiltered and defiant, Drake advocates raw truth over dogma, refusing to sanitize reality.
